-----------------------------
List of Commands
-----------------------------

/help - prints out list of available commands

/tellip - returns current public IP of the Pi

/ifconfig - returns output of ifconfig command, authorization needed

/restartnm - restarts NetworkManager, authorization needed

/bop - sends you a random picture of a doggo! :3

/joke - tells a random joke <!NSFW warning!>

/truth & /dare - returns a random truth or dare challenge, from a list of predefined challenges

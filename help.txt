-----------------------------
List of Commands
-----------------------------

/help - prints out list of available commands

/tech - prints out list of technical commands

/chatid - returns your current Telegram Chat ID!

/hi - says hi

/roll <int> - rolls a dice with <int> sides

/bop - sends you a random picture of a doggo! :3

/joke - tells a random joke

/truth & /dare - returns a random truth or dare challenge, from a list of predefined challenges
